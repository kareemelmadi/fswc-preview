About Our Practice

Comprehensive, Patient-Centered Wound Care

Effective communication and teamwork between patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers is central to our approach, allowing us to deliver coordinated, patient-centered care. By collaborating with primary care physicians, home health services, and specialists, we streamline the treatment process for efficiency and effectiveness.

Rest assured, we will make the appropriate referrals in the event a patient needs additional specialized care, for example, Infectious Disease, Vascular, Podiatric, or General surgery. Our mission extends beyond healing wounds—we strive to educate patients and implement preventive strategies to reduce the likelihood of future wound complications.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about your visit to Full Spectrum Wound Care.

What do I need to bring? +
Any supplies you use for your wound.
How early should I arrive for my first appointment? +
If you have already filled out your forms from the website, 10 minutes before your appointment is ideal. If you have not completed the forms, we recommend you arrive 15–20 minutes before your appointment.
What do I wear for my appointment? +
Comfortable clothing that allows easy access to expose the wound.
What happens if I change insurance? +
Please give us a call or let us know at your next appointment.
When do my supplies arrive? +
Your supplies will arrive within 1 week from your first appointment. If not, please give us a call. We do not send supplies if you have home health.
How often will I need to come? +
Once a week until the wound clears up. Wound care is very patient-specific and your treatment plan is tailored to fit your situation.
