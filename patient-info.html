Your First Visit

What to Expect

Here's what you need to know to prepare for your appointment.

1

Complete Your Paperwork

Fill out the new patient health history questionnaire online or download the PDF. Completing your forms ahead of time saves valuable appointment time.

2

Arrive Early

If you've completed your forms online, arrive 10 minutes before your appointment. If you haven't completed them, we recommend arriving 15–20 minutes early.

3

Bring Your Supplies

Bring any supplies you currently use for your wound. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to expose the wound area.

4

Your Consultation

Our team will assess your wound, discuss your medical history, and develop a personalized treatment plan. We'll explain every step and answer all your questions.
