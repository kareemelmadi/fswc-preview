About Our Practice

Comprehensive Care for Complex Wounds

Full Spectrum Wound Care provides advanced treatment for chronic and complex wounds using evidence-based therapies. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures each patient receives individualized care tailored to their unique needs.

From diabetic ulcers to post-surgical wounds, our team combines years of clinical expertise with the latest healing technologies to deliver outcomes that matter.

Diabetic Wound Care

Pressure Injuries
