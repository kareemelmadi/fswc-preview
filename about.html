Meet Our Team

Our experienced team brings decades of combined expertise in wound care, nursing, and patient-centered medicine.

LW

Dr. Lawrence Wells, M.D.

Physician

Dr. Wells has been in Huntsville since 1986. He has been in solo and group practice in family medicine until retiring in 2023. Since January 2025 he has been working with Full Spectrum Wound Care.

Dr. Wells brings years of experience in treating not only wounds but searching for ways to improve patients' health issues, nutrition, and lifestyle to prevent further troubles.

CO

Cynthia O'Bryant

Patient Care Coordinator

Cynthia serves as the Patient Care Coordinator for Full Spectrum Wound Care, bringing over 35 years of customer service experience to her role. Cynthia takes great pleasure in interacting with patients and ensuring their needs are met.

Her responsibilities include scheduling appointments, greeting patients, taking their vitals, and addressing their needs prior to their appointment with Dr. Wells. Cynthia is excited about the upcoming expansion as the practice continues to grow.

GG

Ginger Greer

M.S.N., R.N., C.W.S., C.O.N.O., C.S.W.D.-C — Wound & Ostomy Specialist

Ginger began her nursing career as a Licensed Vocational Nurse (LVN) in 1999 and continued her education to earn a Master of Science in Nursing degree. In the early years, she worked in long-term care (LTC) facilities and then in acute care medical-surgical, providing nursing care to adult and geriatric populations with acute and chronic conditions.

In 2006, she discovered the world of wound care and realized that was where she was meant to be. She obtained board certification from the American Board of Wound Management as a Certified Wound Specialist (CWS) in 2007. Since then, she has practiced as a wound specialist in several different healthcare fields: wound consultant for LTC, SNF, Assisted Living, and hospice; home health; acute care; and outpatient wound care. She is occasionally contacted to consult on legal cases with a wound care component.

Those different arenas have provided Ginger with a broad knowledge base and deep understanding of wound healing and treatment strategies, and unparalleled hands-on skills. She currently works for Full Spectrum Wound Care, where she shares her knowledge and passion for wound care with patients, families, co-workers, and licensed providers.

"We have a saying in wound care: to treat the whole patient, not just the hole in the patient. That's what we do here." — Ginger Greer
PW

Perri Winston

MSN, RN, AGNP-PC — Nurse Practitioner

Perri is new to Full Spectrum Wound Care and new to the Huntsville area since 2024. She began her career as a registered nurse in 1988, working in the operating room and then home health care. She eventually landed in intensive care nursing and spent the majority of her career in critical care settings. She worked in emergency care and coronary care units for several years, and then in the cardiac cath lab working with cardiac patients in both emergency presentations and diagnostic procedures.

These experiences encouraged her to further her education and she became a nurse practitioner in 2014. She worked for 11 years in a cardiology clinic, seeing patients both in the clinic and the hospital. She became well versed in diagnostics and medications frequently used in heart disease and peripheral vascular disease.

After moving to the Huntsville area in 2024, she worked in hospice care and then came to wound care. Her experience provides a broad spectrum of knowledge that has transferred well to wound care. She finds great enjoyment and satisfaction working with patients and looks forward to continuing to expand her knowledge in wound care.
